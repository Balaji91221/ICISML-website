About Us

About the conference

The 4th International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Machine Learning (ICISML-2024) is a leading event focused on the latest advancements in intelligent systems and machine learning. These technologies play a vital role in analyzing vast amounts of data, aiding decision-making, and driving progress across various domains. By enabling machines to learn from data and perform human-like tasks, they facilitate the discovery of valuable insights and patterns. The conference provides a platform for researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to exchange knowledge, discuss challenges, and explore emerging trends in this dynamic field..
